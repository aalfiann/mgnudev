Description :

From Eternal-Blue: Basically, this manga follows Hibino Hareluya as he tries to “conquer the world” by dealing with the everyday punks on the street. Always ready to fight and always winning his fights, he just finds himself in more and more trouble. Along the way he surrounds himself with friends who are all very strong in their own unique way. It's from the same mangaka of Bremen.
